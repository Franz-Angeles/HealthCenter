Summary Analysis

The Family Planning services have shown consistent growth over the past 12 months, with a 12.5% increase in total consultations compared to the previous year. The most popular methods continue to be hormonal contraceptives (42%) and barrier methods (28%).

Summary Analysis

Immunization services have demonstrated strong growth with a 15.3% increase in total vaccinations administered compared to the previous year. Childhood vaccinations remain the largest segment (68%), followed by adult vaccinations (22%) and specialized immunizations (10%).

The error rate in vaccine administration has decreased by 0.5 percentage points, indicating improved training and protocol adherence. The confidence rate in data accuracy has increased to 97.5%, providing a reliable foundation for predictive analytics.

Based on current trends and seasonal patterns, we predict a 9.5% increase in vaccination demand for the coming month. This increase coincides with the start of the school year and seasonal vaccine campaigns.
