Summary Analysis

Postpartum Care services have shown steady growth with a 9.2% increase in total visits compared to the previous year. This growth reflects increased awareness of the importance of postpartum care and improved access to healthcare services.

The postpartum complication rate has decreased by 1.2 percentage points to 3.8%, indicating improved monitoring and early intervention strategies. Normal recovery rates have increased to 93.5%, showing a 2.1% improvement from the previous year.

Follow-up completion rates have significantly improved to 87.3%, representing a 3.8% increase from the previous year. This improvement can be attributed to better appointment scheduling, reminder systems, and patient education about the importance of postpartum follow-up care.
