Our Mission & Vision

Committed to excellence in healthcare delivery and community wellness

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality healthcare services to all members of our community, focusing on preventive care, health education, and comprehensive medical treatment. We strive to improve the overall health outcomes of our barangays through innovative care delivery and community partnerships.

Our Vision

To be the leading community health center that transforms healthcare delivery through excellence, innovation, and compassionate care. We envision healthy, empowered communities where every individual has access to quality healthcare and the opportunity to live their healthiest life.

Our Healthcare Services

Comprehensive medical care and health programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our community

Primary Care

Comprehensive medical consultations, routine check-ups, and preventive care for all ages.

  • • General medical consultations
  • • Health screenings and assessments
  • • Chronic disease management
  • • Medication management

Maternal & Child Health

Specialized care for mothers and children from pregnancy through early childhood.

  • • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • • Child immunizations
  • • Growth and development monitoring
  • • Family planning services

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency medical care and urgent health services for critical situations.

  • • 24/7 emergency response
  • • First aid and trauma care
  • • Emergency stabilization
  • • Ambulance services

Preventive Care

Comprehensive prevention programs to maintain optimal health and prevent disease.

  • • Annual health check-ups
  • • Vaccination programs
  • • Health education and counseling
  • • Disease prevention screening

Community Health Programs

  • • Disease surveillance programs
  • • Health promotion campaigns

Mental Health Services

  • • Crisis intervention services
